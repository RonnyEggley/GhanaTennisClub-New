About Ghana Tennis Club

Discover our history, mission, and the people who make our club special

Our Story

Ghana Tennis Club is the premier tennis facility in Ghana, dedicated to promoting the sport of tennis and fostering a community of passionate players. Our state-of-the-art facilities and professional coaching staff provide the perfect environment for players of all skill levels.

Established with a vision to develop tennis talent in Ghana and provide world-class facilities, we have grown to become the leading tennis institution in the country. Our club serves as a hub for both recreational and competitive tennis, welcoming players from all backgrounds and abilities.

Professional Coaching

Expert instruction from certified tennis professionals with international experience

Premium Facilities

World-class courts and amenities including modern equipment and comfortable clubhouse

Community Events

Regular tournaments, social activities, and programs for all age groups

Youth Development

Comprehensive junior programs designed to nurture the next generation of tennis talent

Our Mission

To promote tennis excellence, provide exceptional facilities and programs, and foster a vibrant tennis community that develops players at all levels while upholding the highest standards of sportsmanship and integrity.

Our Vision

To be Ghana's leading tennis club, recognized for excellence in player development, community engagement, and promoting tennis as a sport for life, while contributing to the growth of tennis across West Africa.
